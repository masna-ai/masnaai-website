Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about working with us? Here are some common questions and answers.

What types of businesses do you work with?

We work with businesses of all sizes across various industries. Our solutions are particularly valuable for enterprises looking to leverage AI for automation, data analysis, customer engagement, and innovation.

How long does it take to implement an AI solution?

Implementation timelines vary based on the complexity of the solution and your specific requirements. MVP projects can be completed in 4-8 weeks, while more comprehensive enterprise solutions may take 3-6 months.

What is the typical process for starting a project?

We begin with a discovery call to understand your needs, followed by a proposal outlining our approach, timeline, and costs. Once approved, we start with a detailed planning phase before moving into development and implementation.

Do you offer ongoing support after implementation?

Yes, we provide comprehensive support and maintenance options for all our solutions. We also offer training for your team to ensure they can effectively use and manage the AI systems we implement.
